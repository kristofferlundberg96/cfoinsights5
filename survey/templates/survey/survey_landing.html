CFO Insights 2018
International Survey

The CFO Insights 2018 International Survey is a collaboration between students of Copenhagen Business School and KPMG. The aim of the survey is to identify opportunities and challenges on the CFO agenda. We invite you to contribute with your experiences as a CFO by answering this short 10 minute survey. The results of the survey will be emailed to all participants who submit their e-mail. As a token of our gratitude, we're also offering participants a 15% discount on tickets to the 2018 CFO Insights conference at Marriott Copenhagen May 25th where the results will be presented and discussed.
