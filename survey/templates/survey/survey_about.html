About this survey

CFO Insights was established by Danish students from Copenhagen Business School to address the radical transformation currently facing the role of the CFO. Our mission is to arm the region's CFOs for the future and offer them a peak through the looking glass at what the future holds.

We arrange the yearly CFO Insights conference based in Copenhagen with the region's leading service providers, 48 speakers, 9 panel debates and 500 executives, top finance students and thought leaders.

We're grateful for the support by KPMG whose involvement has ensured the quality of the survey's questions.

We hope you'll spend 10 minutes to complete our questions and as a reward, you'll unlock discounted tickets for our conference on May 25th.
